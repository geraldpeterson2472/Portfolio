Web Host and Domain Recommendations

Currently, PipeDownRecords.com is available and can be purchased for as low as $0.99. I have used BluHost for several domains and personally like their business model and pricing. You would be able to get a domain up and running with them for a year, have domain privacy, backup protection and a few other features (depending on your preferences) for a price range of $150 - $200 per year. This also ensures that your domain is on a server that is kept up and running and you have dedicated space for your website.
