Money Matters

During these tough economic times, we can use all the help we can get to make good financial choices. The information found on this site is intended to do just that.


No matter how much or how little you're paid, you'll never get ahead if you spend more than you earn. Often it's easier to spend less than it is to earn more, and a little cost-cutting effort in a number of areas can result in big savings. It doesn't always have to involve making big sacrifices.

You will find links to several online resources on our Links page. Please contact us if you have questions or would like to speak to someone on our staff.
